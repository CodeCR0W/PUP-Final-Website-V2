About Us

Welcome to the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Quezon City Campus, a pioneering institution committed to excellence in education and community service.

We Are the Country's 1st PolytechnicU

The PUP Quezon City Campus, formerly PUP Commonwealth Campus, is a 1.9-hectare forested land area assigned, transferred, and endorsed to the Polytechnic University of the Philippines by the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council.

Started as petitions to have a higher educational institution for the youth at the National Government Center area from the community organizations and church groups, namely; Parokya ng Mabuting Pastol, Kaagapay sa Makatarungan at Pantay na Paninirahan, Inc., SUPERBLOCKS, and Kristong Hari Foundation.

The campus is committed to providing quality education to urban poor communities of Quezon City and its neighboring localities.
